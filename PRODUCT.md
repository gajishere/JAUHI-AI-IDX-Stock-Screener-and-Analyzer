# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Indonesian retail traders analyzing IDX (Indonesia Stock Exchange) stocks — primarily the owner, who shares results with a trading community (Telegram/Discord circles). Used during and after market hours to read foreign flows, broker summaries (bandarmology), and technical/fundamental signals, then turn them into concrete trading plans. Output is frequently screenshotted and shared, so report readability is a first-class requirement.

## Product Purpose

A two-surface analysis tool for IDX stocks:

1. **Stock Analysis** — deep single-ticker report: foreign flow, broker analysis, technical, fundamental, trend, timeframe ratings (short/mid/long), rationale, action recommendations, and key price levels.
2. **Stock Screening** — daily workflow that surfaces 5 candidate stocks (closing or mid-day mode), enriches them with uploaded broker-summary screenshots, and produces a final priority ranking with trading plans.

Success looks like: a trader can run the workflow in minutes, trust what they read, and share a report screenshot that looks like professional research.

## Brand Personality

Calm, analytical, deliberate. The feel of a well-typeset equity research report, not a trading terminal. Reduces adrenaline rather than amplifying it — built for considered decisions. Three words: **measured, literate, trustworthy**.

## Anti-references

- **Bloomberg-terminal clutter**: no wall-to-wall data density, no ALL-CAPS shouting everywhere, no ten colors fighting for attention.
- Crypto-hype aesthetics (neon glows, gamified urgency) and generic indigo SaaS dashboard templates are also out of character.

## Design Principles

1. **Read like research, not like a feed** — typographic hierarchy does the work; the page should feel authored.
2. **Numbers are the content** — tabular figures, aligned columns, generous breathing room around data.
3. **Color is a verdict, not decoration** — green/red/amber appear only where they carry meaning (direction, rating, risk).
4. **Screenshot-ready by default** — any report section cropped and shared should stand alone legibly.
5. **One calm surface** — light, paper-adjacent ground; restraint over spectacle.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA contrast (≥4.5:1 body text), full keyboard navigability, visible focus states, `prefers-reduced-motion` support. Gains/losses carry +/− signs in addition to color.
